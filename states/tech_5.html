Top Accounting Softwares for Small Businesses

When you’re a small business that is just starting out, it isn’t always possible to hire a team of seasoned accountants to handle your finances. Instead, you can always consider trying out accounting software to help keep track of your accounts receivable and accounts payable, get a clear understanding of your profitability, and the most important aspect, be prepared for tax season. 

There are many different kinds of accounting software available in the market that come with various capabilities and price ranges. It is important for you to find software that fits your business’s needs like a glove. Two factors that you should take into consideration while choosing your future accounting software are the type of industry you function in and the number of employees you have.

Here are our top 4 softwares after careful comparison in terms of ease of use, features offered, cost, ease, and scalability:

QuickBooks Online- An Overall Favorite

QuickBooks Online is the best overall accounting software for small businesses. Why? Because if you are just starting out, QuickBooks Online, offers endless online training resources and support from a team of experts. Its easy-to-use interface allows the user to easily access all accounting features from one main dashboard and makes bookkeeping more smooth and efficient.

Wave- Best Free Software in the Market

Wave is ideal for service-based small businesses whose needs include sending simple invoices and not inventory tracking or running payroll. If you are a freelance or service-based business, we are confident that Wave’s free features will be more than enough to cover your accounting needs. You can also easily pull out all the necessary reports to file your business’ tax return.

FreshBooks – Top Pick for Service-Based Businesses

For a service-based business, the most crucial accounting need is invoicing. FreshBooks offers the maximum number of invoicing customizations compared to any other software. This software’s primary function is to assist you in sending, receiving, storing/filing/printing, and paying invoices. It also takes care of your bookkeeping needs.

QuickBooks Self-Employed- Great for Part-Time Freelancers 

QuickBooks Self-Employed accounting software is our top recommendation for independent consultants and part-time freelancers whose primary need is to keep a track of their expenses & income for tax season. 

If you are someone who finds bookkeeping to be a daunting challenge, QuickBooks Self-Employed is the perfect solution.
