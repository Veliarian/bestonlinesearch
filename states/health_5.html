Top 8 Air Purifiers Compared

Air purifiers at home can help ensure that the air you breathe is clean. They have been proven to improve air quality and actually help you breathe easier. And these are the best of the lot.

Blue Pure 211+ Air Purifier

This air purifier is best for removing dust and smoke from the air. It has an effective three-part filtration system that claims to clean 99% of common airborne pollutants. It’s also very quiet, which makes it ideal for bedrooms and home offices.

Blue Pire 411 Air Purifier

This is a smaller, more affordable version of the one above. It offers great value while providing the same level of purification as its larger counterpart. It’s best for rooms between 100 to 175 square feet.

Pure Cool Air Purifier and Tower Fan

This Dyson air purifier uses a HEPA filter to clear 99.97% of airborne pollutants, odors, and toxins. It has no accessible blades, so it is safe around children. It has a nighttime mode and a sleep timer too.

Airmega 400 Smart Air Purifier

This purifier from Coway claims to cover up to 1,560 square feet. It cleans the air twice per hour and reduces 99.97% of particles. You can choose from five different modes, including a sleep setting.

True HEPA Allergen Remover Air Purifier

This HEPA-Certified Honeywell purifier comes in five different sizes that target rooms from 75 square feet to 465 square feet. It cleans the air five times per hour and removes 99.97% of airborne particles.

Mighty Air Purifier

This Coway purifier has excellent reviews from over one thousand six hundred customers. It has an LED light that indicates how clean or dirty the air is. It also purifies the air up to 361 feet and even has various fan speeds, including an energy-saving mode.

Pure Hot + Cool Air Purifier

This versatile Dyson machine is a heater, cooler, and purifier all in one. It also syncs with Amazon Alexa through your Echo device and can deliver real-time air quality reports through the Dyson app.

Healthmate Standard Air Purifier B400B1

This Austin Air purifier has a true HEPA filter to capture particulate pollutants efficiently. It also has a filter that captures gaseous pollutants like VOCs.
