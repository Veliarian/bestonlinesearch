Check Out The Latest Phones This Year

Want to buy a new phone? Well, you’re in luck because there are some amazing smartphones out there that offer incredible features and great value. Here are some of the latest offerings.

iPhone 12 Pro Max

The iPhone 12 Pro Max is probably the best phone you can purchase right now. This is one of those rare phones that are practically perfect, especially for people who prefer large screens. You get a 6.7-inch OLED display, top-notch A14 Bionic performance, and 5G capability. The battery life is amazing as well.

Like the regular iPhone 12 Pro, the 12 Pro Max features three rear cameras, but it benefits from a sensor that lets in more light. Additionally, you get a 2.5 telephoto zoom, compared to the 2x that the Pro has. The iPhone 12 Pro Max also offers top-quality video recording in Dolby Vision. At $1,099, this model of the iPhone is not cheap, but it’s one of the best phones out there right now.

Samsung Galaxy S21 Ultra

If you’re looking for a big-screen phone, the Samsung Galaxy S21 Ultra is a perfect choice. It offers you a 6.8-inch AMOLED display with a refresh rate that scales from 10Hz to 120Hz for extra smooth performance. But, the best part about this phone is its dual telephoto cameras. The camera quality has also improved.

We also love the performance that the Snapdragon 888 chip offers and the phone’s extra-long battery life. One of the only downsides is that the Galaxy S21 Ultra does not come with a charger. We also wish it had a microSD slot. But, otherwise, it’s one of the best Android phones you can purchase.

Google Pixel 4a

What makes this smartphone so special is that it offers you premium features for a very affordable price – $349. It’s built with a gorgeous OLED screen that measures 5.8 inches, gets solid power from the Snapdragon 730G chipset, and has a number of clever software features like Google’s extremely handy Recorder live transcription app, a Call Screen, and a 12-megapixel camera that offers you pers like Super Res Zoom and Night Sight.

The only area where this phone doesn’t deliver is the battery life – it does last long, but not as long as its rival, the iPhone SE. But, it’s practical in pretty much every other way, and we love this well-rounded handset.
