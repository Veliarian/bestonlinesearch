Comparing Small Business Software Solutions

If you’re looking for business management software for your small business, we’ve got a list of some very interesting options. These should help keep your systems working even when you start growing and add new processes and departments.

NetSuite

This is the world’s best cloud business solution. It provides a unified platform that streamlines all back-office processes like accounting, CRM, and eCommerce. It features built-in business intelligence, reporting, and real-time cross-organization visibility.

Jira

This is the software development tool of choice for agile teams of two to 20,000. It is used to manage each stage of the workflow, from planning to shipping and releasing. Teams can use native roadmaps to ladder up to their work and track the big picture. Its no-code system allows teams to save time and amplify power by automating tasks easily.

Bitrix24

This total business management software is completely free. You also get 35 free tools, including CRM, tasks, document management, time management, business process automation, project management, invoicing, collaboration, email marketing, absence management, company directory, virtual PBX, and a lot more.

Todo.vu

This productivity tool offers a new blend of time tracking, task management, contact management, and billing functionality. It’s great for freelancers since it’s totally free for them, and all pricing plans are capped at the cost of 11 users. It’s also good for consultants and teams of all sizes that want more efficiency, quality, and transparency.

Averox Business Management

This is best for small and medium-scale businesses in any industry. This software can also serve nonprofits, governments, and all sizes of businesses. It has a lot of features like CRM, legal eSigning, Accounting, Inventory, Task & Document Management, eCampaigns, stock management, and more. It even includes free Android and iOS apps with no subscription.

ProWorkflow

This is a leading comprehensive project management tool for teams from five to 5000. The company also has 17 years of experience and has been trusted for over three million projects. It knows how to manage the small details and handle big data. It has a powerful API and customizable features that let users tailor this software to their needs. There’s also world-class customer support and free onboarding that should make signing up for this service rather easy.
